**RiverRage** is an [off-season competition](/index.php/Off-season_competition "Off-season competition" ) hosted at Manchester West high school, in Manchester, New Hampshire and is attended by many [teams](/index.php/FIRST_Robotics_team "FIRST Robotics team" ). Founded in 1997 by the Manchester West [(Team 501)](/index.php/501 "501" ), Central and Memorial high schools, it is the oldest and longest running post-season competition. 

RiverRage 10 will be held at the site of the 1992 and 1995 event; Memorial
High School in Manchester, New Hampshire on October 28, 2006. More information
can be found [at the official FIRST
website.](http://www.usfirst.org/robotics/res_art4.htm
"http://www.usfirst.org/robotics/res_art4.htm" )
