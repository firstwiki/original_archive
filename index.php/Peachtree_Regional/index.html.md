The **Peachtree Regional** is a FIRST Robotics competition that takes place in
March. Since 2003, teams from all over the country come to participate in one
of FIRST's fastest growing events.

  
The Peachtree Regional is held at the Gwinnett Civic and Cultural Center in
Duluth, Georgia.
