## History

Founded in 1999. 30-40 active members. Wonderful sponsors. Senior team from
Washington State!


## Team projects

We are active in the community with many community events, Habitat for
Humanity, and of course [FLL](FLL "FLL" ).


## Robots

  * Rainmaker I -- 1999-2000 
  * Rainmaker II -- 2000-2001 
  * Rainmaker Pi -- 2001-2002 
  * Rainmaker IV -- 2002-2003 
  * Rainmaker V -- 2003-2004 


## Accomplishments

  * 2004 PNW Woodie Flowers Award -- Paul Roush Team **360**
  * 2004 OR GM Industrial Design 
  * 2002 WA Imagery 
  * 2000 CA Best Offensive Round
