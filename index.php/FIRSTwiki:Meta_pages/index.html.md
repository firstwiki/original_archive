The FIRST MetaWiki is the section of [FIRSTwiki](/index.php/FIRSTwiki
"FIRSTwiki" ) that describes the main wiki. Just as meta-data gives
information about data, the MetaWiki gives information, such as
[help](/index.php/FIRSTwiki:Help "FIRSTwiki:Help" ), about
[FIRSTwiki](/index.php/FIRSTwiki "FIRSTwiki" ). Meta pages' titles begin with
"FIRSTwiki:" or "MediaWiki:".

All relevant meta pages are linked to from the [Meta
TOC](/index.php/Template:FIRSTwiki_meta_toc "Template:FIRSTwiki meta toc" ).
When creating or editting meta pages, be sure to add:

    
    
    {{FIRSTwiki_meta_toc}}
    

at the top of the page.
