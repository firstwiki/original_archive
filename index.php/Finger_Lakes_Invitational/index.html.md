The **Finger Lakes Invitational** is an [off-season competition](/index.php
/Off-season_competition "Off-season competition") that began in 2004.

It was started as a precursor to the [Finger Lakes Regional](Finger_Lakes_Regional "Finger Lakes Regional"), both to attract potential sponsors, and, more importantly, to attract local schools.

In 2004, There were eight teams from Rochester competing. Team [211](211 "211") seeded first, [191](191 "191") seeded second, [340](340 "340") seeded third, and [1405](1405 "1405") seeded fourth. Team 211 picked 191 while team 340 chose 1405, eschewing 1126 who they were [Regional Champions](Regional_Champion "Regional Champion") and [Division Champions](/index.php?title=Division_Champion&action=edit "Division Champion") with. The 191 and 211 [alliance](Alliance "Alliance") had over 20 years of combined [FIRST](first) experience, whereas the 340 and 1405 alliance had only five. The finals consisted of a single match, which the 340 and 1405 alliance won 120 to 115, with all four [robots](Robot "Robot") hanging.
