In 2004, The Oregon [Regional](Regional "Regional" ) (formerly, the
Pacific Northwest Regional) was held in Portland, Oregon. In 2002 (its
inception) and 2003, it was held in Seattle, Washington. This year (2008), the
regional will be held from 2/28/08 - 3/1/08 at the Memorial Coliseum in
Portland, Oregon. The official website for the Pacific Northwest Regional is
[[1]](http://www.pnwfirst.org/ "http://www.pnwfirst.org/" )
