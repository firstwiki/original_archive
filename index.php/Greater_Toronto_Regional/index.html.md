The **Greater Toronto Regional**, formerly known as the FIRST Canadian Regional, began in 2002, and has been run at the Hershey Centre in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada (a suburb of Toronto, Ontario, Canada). In 2004 it became the first regional to EVER have more than 1 field due to massive demand. In 2005, GTR continued to have 2 fields, as well as the Waterloo Regional starting only about an hour's drive away in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. Beginning in 2006, Greater Toronto Regional has returned to only having 1 field.

As of 2011, there are now two fields once again, GTR West and GTR East.
