**San Diego Scrimmage** is an [off-season competition](Off-season_competition "Off-season competition") that began in 2004\.

In 2004, there was a lock-in the Friday night before competition, to give teams a chance to bond. The Champions, teams [330](330 "330") and [980](980 "980"), tied the highest score at the time, 230.
